Raspberry Kefir Overnight Oatmeal

Description

A simple and delicious ready-to-go breakfast option.

Ingredients

  • 125ml milk 1.5%
  • 50gr kefir yoghurt
  • 40gr oats
  • 6 raspberries, crushed
  • 1 tsp chocolate drops
  • 1 tsp chia seeds

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients into a mason jar and mix well.
  2. Put lid on mason jar and put in refrigerator for at least 4 hours, ideally overnight.
  3. Enjoy!
